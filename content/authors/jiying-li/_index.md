Jiying Li completed B.E. in Chemical Engineering at East China University of Science and Technology and M.S. in Chemical Engineering at Northwestern University. She joined Bioelectronics group as a PhD student in MIT DMSE. Before joining the lab, she worked on 3D meso-structured bioelectronics for organoid neural interface. Her current research focuses on developing soft, stretchable multimodal platforms in physiological systems. Outside the lab, she enjoys swimming and playing billiards.
